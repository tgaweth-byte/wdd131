Website Planning Document

Discovering Haiti - Heritage and Culture

1. Site Name

Haiti Cultural Heritage

Domain: haiticulturalheritage.org (Available)

Reason for choice: This name clearly communicates the website's purpose: to showcase Haiti's rich cultural heritage. It's descriptive, memorable, and includes keywords that help with search engine optimization. The ".org" extension emphasizes the educational and non-commercial nature of the site.

2. Site Purpose

The website serves as a comprehensive digital resource that:

  • Educates visitors about Haiti's unique history as the world's first Black republic
  • Showcases the country's rich cultural traditions, art, music, and cuisine
  • Provides accurate geographical and climate information about different regions
  • Offers practical tourism information for potential visitors
  • Counters common misconceptions and stereotypes about Haiti
  • Serves as a connection point for the Haitian diaspora worldwide

3. Scenarios

Questions from target audience members:

Tourist Visitor:

"I'm planning a trip to Haiti next year. What are the must-see historical sites, and what safety precautions should I take?"

Student Researcher:

"I'm writing a paper about Caribbean history. Where can I find reliable information about Haiti's independence and its impact on the region?"

Diaspora Member:

"My grandparents were from Haiti, but I've never visited. What cultural traditions should I learn about to connect with my heritage?"

Teacher:

"I want to teach my students about Haiti's cultural contributions. What resources and multimedia content do you have available for classroom use?"
